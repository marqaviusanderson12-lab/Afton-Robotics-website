About Us

At Afton Robotics, LLC, we specialize in the design and development of cutting-edge animatronic systems for the family entertainment and restaurant industries. With decades of experience in advanced robotics and AI-driven performance technology, we are proud to be at the forefront of interactive entertainment solutions. Our animatronic characters are engineered with precision, featuring lifelike movement, dynamic voice integration, and facial recognition capabilities to create memorable guest experiences. Whether it’s live performance venues or themed dining environments, our products are built to delight audiences of all ages.
